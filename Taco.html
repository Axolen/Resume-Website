Tacopacolypse

Tacopacolypse is a fast-paced delivery game where you deliver tacos to hungry customers while the world is coming to its end. It has a fun 90's vibe that was awesome to work on.

This was an exciting project to work on! I produced sounds for car crashes, burning rubber, customization sounds & much more! I even made music for the title as well.

This required editing car smashing sound effects which was really fun to record. With the help of the programming team, we were able to make a sound system that worked beautifully when the player hit any object!

The team was amazing to work with! Jeremy Boggs, Berry David, Brandon Austin, Christina Parker were all awesome!
